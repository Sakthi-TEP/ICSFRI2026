Call for Papers — ICSFRI 2026

Submit your original research papers and review articles for presentation at the First International Conference on Sustainable Futures through Responsible Innovation — 27–28 February 2026 (Online)

Submission Guidelines

  • Maximum length: 12 pages (single-column format)
  • Prolonged literature reviews are not accepted
  • Review articles with expanded findings and comparative analyses are welcome

Template & Format

Manuscript templates are available in .docx and .pdf formats in the Downloads section of the conference website.

Submissions Should Include

  • Novel research concepts
  • Ideas for further development
  • Detailed analyses, conclusions, and outcomes

Note: Manuscripts must not be under review or published elsewhere.

Author Information

  • Names and affiliations of all authors
  • Name and email of the corresponding author

Submission Instructions

Prepare manuscripts in the required format and send soft copies in both .docx and .pdf formats via email to: icsfri2026@gmail.com

Conference Topics

Environmental Sciences

  • Air and Water Pollution Monitoring and Mitigation
  • Climate Change Adaptation and Resilience Strategies
  • Advances in Waste Management and Recycling Technologies
  • Biodiversity Conservation and Restoration
  • Remote Sensing and GIS Applications in Environmental Studies

Sustainable Materials & Innovation

  • Renewable Energy and Resource Management
  • Advanced Functional Materials
  • Composite Materials
  • Nanomaterials and Nanotechnology
  • Smart Cities and Sustainable Infrastructure
  • Digital Transformation in Agriculture and Industry
  • Circular Economy and Resource Efficiency
  • AI and Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Solutions

Ready to Submit?

Prepare your manuscript in the required format and email the soft copies to icsfri2026@gmail.com. Make sure to include all author details and follow the submission guidelines.
