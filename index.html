Conference Overview

The ICSFRI 2026 aims to unite students, scholars, and researchers to share innovative ideas on sustainable development and responsible innovation. It provides an interdisciplinary platform to showcase impactful research supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

This conference fosters knowledge exchange across science, technology, environment, and social responsibility — leading toward a sustainable future.
